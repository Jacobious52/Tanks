=====================================

Usage ./game <args>

./game (no args) just play

--skip
Skip intro screen

--noaudio
No audio will play

--debug
show debugging view
use this flag when piping computer.txt

--rain
turns on rain effect

--gametime <float>
sets game tick speed to <float>

--replay
plays replay from piped into input usually replay.txt
can be another saved file
do not use with --skip
